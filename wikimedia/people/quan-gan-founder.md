## Entrepreneurial Achievements
### ZTAG (2016-Present)
Created a physical reality game system that brings elements from online gaming into the physical world, encouraging kids to engage in active play with teamwork and strategy. The platform promotes social, physical, and mental fitness for children through technology-enabled face-to-face interactions.
### Gantom Lighting & Controls (2010-Present)
Co-founded and owns a specialty lighting company that manufactures the world's smallest DMX controllable spotlights. Products are used across multiple industries including theme parks, museums, entertainment venues, and landscaping. Systems have been installed at major theme parks worldwide.
### Other Ventures

## Innovation & Patents
Quan is an inventor at the forefront of interactive technology. He was recently awarded US Patent 12337234B2 for the "System and method of using a virtual focal point in real physical game," the foundational technology behind ZTAG's real-time, visible, and interactive play system. This breakthrough enables seamless synchronization of digital and physical gameplay, creating a distinct and easily followed "virtual focal point" during group games, benefitting both players and spectators.

In addition to his work in interactive play, Quan also holds several patents in lighting control systems and user interfaces, including:
- Control system with user interface for lighting fixtures
- System and Method for Communicating with and for Controlling of Programmable Apparatuses
- System and method of control of a programmable lighting fixture with embedded memory
