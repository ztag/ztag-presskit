# ZTAG Brand Colors & Typography

## Primary Colors
- **#0256A0**  
- **#39c2d6**  
- **#62bb46**  
- **#FAC224**

## Secondary Colors
- **#00a3e0**  
- **#f9423a**  
- **#ffffff** (white)  
- **#000000** (black)

*Additional CMYK color values are defined in the full brand guide.*

---

## Typography
**Primary Typeface: Montserrat**

- **Bold:** for headings and callouts  
- **Regular:** for body content and general use
- A–Z, a–z, 0–9 glyph support

---

## Logo Usage
- Always use the official red-to-blue gradient logo with a white border.
- Do not stretch, recolor, distort, or alter the logo's proportions.
- Specific single-color and gradient variants should be used according to background color (see brand guidelines).

For more design guidance, refer to the [Full Brand Guidelines PDF](https://www.ztag.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/FINAL-ZTAG-Brand-Guidelines-4.pdf).
