## Extended Version (~240 words)

ZTAG, founded by entrepreneur and engineer Quan Gan, is a revolutionary tech-enabled active learning platform that empowers children to connect, grow, and thrive through meaningful face-to-face play experiences. The platform uses innovative wearable devices and interactive game mechanics to encourage teamwork, physical movement, STEM learning, and social-emotional development in a fun, inclusive, and engaging environment.

Unlike traditional educational technology that often isolates students behind screens, ZTAG bridges the gap between digital engagement and real-world interaction. The system creates immersive learning experiences that integrate STEM and social-emotional learning (SEL) principles through active gameplay, helping students build cognitive skills, confidence, and collaborative problem-solving abilities while staying physically active and socially connected.

ZTAG's portable, scalable design makes it straightforward to deploy across diverse educational settings including schools, summer camps, after-school programs, and community centers. The platform supports learners across various backgrounds and developmental needs, including neurodiverse children, and works seamlessly both indoors and outdoors. With minimal setup requirements and no reliance on Wi-Fi connectivity, ZTAG removes common technology barriers while maximizing accessibility and ease of implementation.

The platform transforms abstract educational concepts into tangible, memorable experiences that foster joy, connection, and lifelong learning. By combining the excitement of interactive gaming with proven educational methodologies, ZTAG creates an environment where children naturally develop essential 21st-century skills through play. Visit ztag.com to discover how ZTAG is revolutionizing active learning and bringing kids together in meaningful ways.
